Our Process

Step 1: Assessment of Eligibility

At Cheshire Property Advisers, we begin the process by inviting clients to upload a copy of their business rates bill. This crucial first step allows us to thoroughly assess their eligibility for small business rate relief. By examining the details provided, we can determine the potential for reducing their business rates and explore available relief options.

Step 2: Preliminary Analysis and Response

Following the submission of the business rates bill, our expert team conducts a thorough analysis. We carefully review the information provided and promptly respond to the client, informing them whether their case qualifies for further assistance. This transparent communication ensures our clients are well-informed about the potential opportunities for rate relief before proceeding to the next steps.

Step 3: On-Site Property Visit

For cases that show promise, we aim to provide personalised service by arranging an appointment to visit the client's property. During this on-site visit, our team captures essential details, conducts a property assessment, and takes photographs. This step allows us to gather comprehensive information and strengthens our position when negotiating with the local authorities on behalf of our clients.

Step 4: Council Interaction and Refund Process

Once we've gathered the necessary information, Cheshire Property Advisers takes the lead in engaging with the local council. Our team advocates on behalf of our clients, working diligently to secure the maximum rate relief or refund possible. We navigate the complexities of the council processes, ensuring a smooth and efficient interaction that ultimately results in financial benefits for our clients.
